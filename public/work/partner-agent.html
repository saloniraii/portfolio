THE SETUP

40M partner users on the platform. Most of them never log in.

Partner Cloud manages the resellers and distributors who drive 75% of enterprise B2B revenue. A massive, high-value product — with an engagement crisis no amount of portal polish could fix.

8 days
Average support-case resolution — information scattered across systems.
11%
Of cases ended with a manager sending a help link the partner could've found themselves.
500
Partners each channel manager engages every month. The math never worked through a portal.
Insight 01

The portal assumed captive attention

81% of the customer base (Scale-up + Micro-SaaS) had no dedicated channel managers to drive adoption. For them, the portal was a sunk cost.

Insight 02

Partners already had a workspace

WhatsApp, Slack, SMS. That's where deals moved and questions got asked. The portal was a side quest.

Insight 03

The jobs were conversational

Check pricing. Register a deal. Ask about MDF. These aren't GUI workflows — they're questions. The exact shape of a conversation.

THE VISION — MEET PARTNERS WHERE THEY WORK

Stop bringing partners to us.
Go to them.

Instead of improving the portal-based agent, I proposed a headless, omnichannel partner agent — one identity operating natively across every messaging surface, backed by the full power of Salesforce partner management.

HEADLESS

No GUI dependency.

OMNICHANNEL

Same agent, same context, same capabilities — whichever platform the partner prefers. Context-aware and surface-aware.

TOPIC 01 · THE HARDEST FLOW

A 15-field form, re-authored as dialogue

On the portal, deal registration is picklists, date pickers, currency inputs and uploads. I translated the entire workflow into natural turns — each answer intelligently mapped to the correct Salesforce object field. The partner never sees a form. They just have a conversation, and a fully valid record appears in their vendor's CRM.

Partner Agent · WhatsApp · Deal Registration
Let's register this deal. Who's the customer?
Meridian Logistics
Got it. Which product are they interested in?
SmartSolar Pro, ~60 units
And when do you expect to close?
end of next month
✔ Done! Deal registered to Meridian Logistics. You'll get a decision within 5 business days.

From form to conversation

A 15-field CRM form compressed into 6 natural turns. Each response is intelligently mapped to the right object field — partner never touches a dropdown, date picker, or submit button.

→ Valid Deal Registration record created in vendor CRM

NARRATIVE DATA PRESENTATION

When you can't draw a table, you talk it

A pricing table can't render on WhatsApp — so the numbers become a sentence, with tier logic and a call to action folded in. Information design without a canvas.

TOPIC 02 · MDF ENQUIRIES

Market Development Funds

what's my MDF balance?
Your MDF summary:
Allocated: $40,000
Committed: $12,500
Available: $27,500

⚠️ $8,000 expires in 24 days — want to plan a campaign?
A raw number is useless without context. The response always summarizes allocation, committed, available — plus a proactive expiry warning. Replaces a portal page that took 4 clicks and 3 views to assemble.
TOPIC 03 · AUTHENTICATION

In-thread 2FA

show me my open deals
Quick check first — I've sent a 6-digit code to your registered email. Pop it in here?
418 302
✔ Verified! Here are your 3 open deals...
The hardest challenge: partners on WhatsApp aren't logged in. I designed mid-conversation 2FA that never breaks the thread — no redirect, no new window, no broken context.
TOPIC 04 · ERROR RECOVERY

Graceful degradation

$50K-ish
Got it — around $50K. To submit, I'll need the exact figure. What should I put down?
$48,000
Perfect — logging $48,000.
Messaging has no form validation. Re-prompts feel natural, not robotic — the conversation never crashes on "next Tuesday" or "$50K-ish."
